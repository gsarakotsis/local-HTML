AI in FIFA Games throughout the years

The early editions of the FIFA series primarily focused on basic gameplay mechanics and graphics. AI opponents were relatively simple, with limited strategic decision-making.As technology advanced in the 2000s, so did the sophistication of AI in FIFA games. Developers implemented more realistic player movements, improved tactical AI for both offensive and defensive play, as well as introduced features like dynamic difficulty adjustment to make matches more competitive and engaging. In 2010 FIFA incorporated AI-driven features for player personalization. This included improved player behavior and responsiveness, creating a more immersive gaming experience. Also since FIFA transitioned to the Frostbite game engine in FIFA 17, EA started introducing enhanced graphics and more realistic player animations.
